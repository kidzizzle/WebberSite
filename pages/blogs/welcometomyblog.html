At the time of starting this website I'm learning computer science at a University. I'm not sure if I like it, but I'm too far in to give up now. It's not that computers are boring to me (in fact I don't think they ever will be), I'm just not sure I'm a fan of hyperfocusing on minor details to optimize memory usage and efficiency.

I'd much rather spend my time talking about cooler things like music and movies and video games and having fun. There's so much to see and do in the world and it gets overwhelming, but I think that's where you find the most beauty.

I want to learn another language. I want to travel to foreign countries. I want to meet people that I'd never be friends with. I want to hear people's stories and talk to them. Some day I think I'll be able to do these things, but I'm not sure when and I wish it was now.

I'm sure this won't be the last time I talk about this on here, but idk how often I'll get personal on here, since this is the internet and I don't fw revealing myself online.
